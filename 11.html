Ghost Rankings

#11: Citrus

Rating: 5/10

Look, we had to start somewhere. Citrus is not a bad flavour. It beats the hell out of Red Bull and Monster. But it's just... boring. It's a fine flavour, but it cannot compete with Sprite in terms of a lemon lime flavoured drink. I'm not mad at it, but I'm not reaching for it in a fridge with other flavours of Ghost.
